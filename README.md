Hypothesis-Gate is a full-stack web application designed to manage academic research ideas through a structured and secure digital workflow. The platform enables students to submit research ideas, mentors to review and evaluate them, and administrators to manage users and assignments efficiently.

In many academic environments, research ideas are shared through informal methods such as emails or documents, leading to poor tracking, lack of transparency, and inefficient evaluation. Hypothesis-Gate solves this by providing a centralized system for idea submission, mentor review, and progress monitoring.

 Key Features

 Structured research idea submission (text and document formats)

 Mentor evaluation with feasibility and similarity scoring

 Role-based access for students, mentors, and administrators

 Secure real-time communication between students and mentors

 Status tracking (pending, reviewed, approved, rejected)

 Administrative user and mentor management
